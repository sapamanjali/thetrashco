What is EPR Compliance?
Imagine the circumstance where products in the market and the waste-generated from it are properly channelised instead of ending in a waste dump or landfills.

EPR Compliance is a MANDATE for Producers, Importers and Brand Owners(PIBOs) to channelise the plastic Packaging waste, e-waste, battery waste and tyre waste generated to registered Recyclers for environmentally sound management of waste.

According to EPR Guideline, issued by CPCB under Fourth Amendment to Plastic Waste Management Rules 2016, it is now compulsory for entities to get certified and fulfil their liabilities.

In other words, Extended Producers’ Responsibility(EPR) is an OPPORTUNITY that puts the power of sustainability and waste management in your hands.
