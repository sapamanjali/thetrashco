Environmental Services
Our core expertise doesn't end with consultancy but is extended towards operations and project implementations on a large scale basis. The demands of ESG including Carbon Mapping, Life Cycle Assements, BRSR will be met with our operational expertise on how we can together meet these demands through projects such as Green Building Certifications (LEED, IGBC), ISO 14001, Product Designs for Improved LCA and Environmental Factors, Sustainable Packaging.
Waste Management
We are logistically present in all the Indian States for Collections of Industrial Waste of All Types and then we ensure the materials are recycled in a compliant manner with essential certifications. Our Tech-Enabled Platform will provide you with a Dashboard that allows you to view materials channelised across all your Plants in India including the transaction details. We focus currently on Plastics, Paper, Metals, Electronics and Electrical Wastes, Mixed Waste.
Recycled Polymers
We support on research, development and supply of Recycled Polymers as per your application requirements, where we build sourcing channels for waste till recycling industries with high quality standards and processes providing a consistent output of recycled polymers with desired percentage of recycled content.
