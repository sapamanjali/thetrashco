Extended Producer's Responsibility (EPR)

EPR is an Environmental Policy where the Producers of the Products must take the responsibility to ensure safe disposal of the products at the End of their Life Cycle

Policy implementation requires on-ground Action, which is spearheaded by our Organisation, by ensuring the Policies are not just adhered to as Compliance by the Companies but rather as Environmental Actions.
